Hello, My name is Jaylyn Magana & I made this page in hopes to share my life with those who are intrested in learning more about me. Enjoy!

Early life:

I was born and raised in San Antonio , Texas. I am the oldest of 3 and grew up on the Southside of town. Living in poverty, I was exposed very early on in life to drugs, violence, and deliquency by my classmates/peers. Most people from my side of town did not care for school or felt that they weren't worthy of any type of higher education. Thankfully , this was not the case for me. My parents did their absolute best to keep me on the right track and made sure I had goals and aspirations beyond grade school. They never gave up on me and I am forever grateful to be given the opportunity to continue pursing my education.
