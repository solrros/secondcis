Welcome to my page!

Hello! My name is Jaylyn Magana & I made this about me page in hopes to share my parts of my life with those who are intrested in learning more about me. Hope you enjoy!

Early life:

I was born and raised in San Antonio , Texas. I am the oldest of 3 and grew up on the Southside of town. Most people from my side of town did not care for school or felt that they weren't worthy of any type of higher education. Thankfully , this was not the case for me. My parents did their absolute best to keep me on the right track and made sure I had goals and aspirations beyond grade school. They never gave up on me and for that , I am forever grateful.

Today:

I am currently Senior at the University of the Incarnate Word in San Antonio, where I study Criminal Justice and Computer Information System. As a first-gen student , my first couple of years as an undergraduate student were hard to navigate and of course, there were some challenges. Despite that , UIW has always made me feel welcomed and i'm glad that I attended a school that surrounds itself with positivity, kindness and community.

Future Plans:

As my final year of undergrad comes to an end, I can't help but be excited to continue my education. For the next few months , I will be solely spending my free time applying for Criminal Justice Master and Doctoral programs that will help me continue to contribute research into the field. I hope to stay in Texas and use the time I have after graduation with my family.
